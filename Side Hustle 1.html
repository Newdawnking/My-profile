Born on 25 June 1980, Apostle Joshua Selman is a man who has dedicated much of his entire life in service to God, raising and nurturing many in the ministry of Jesus. He is the founder of Eternity Network International, a ministry that transforms lives through God’s word. When the Apostle speaks, you’ve just got to listen and none can leave the atmosphere of his ministration without a life-changing experience.
